Shepard's Pie

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef 1 lbs
  • Mashable potatoes 8 oz
  • Grated Cheese (Permasan, chedder, whatever you want, just make sure it melts)
  • Carrots 6 oz
  • Celery 2 stalks
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • Garlic powder 1 tbsp
  • Thyme 1 tbsp, but go crazy if you dare
  • Tomato paste 3 oz
  • Butter 2 oz
  • Beef Broth

  1. Start boiling water, then immerse the cut and skinned potatoes for 15-20 minutes, then drain, add 2 oz of butter, and mash away. Add Thyme for the, cottage vibes.
  2. Cut carrots, onion, and celery. Saute carrots for 2 minutes, then add the other veggies.
  3. Salt and pepper the veggies as desired and after 7 minutes (in heaven), mix in garlic powder and thyme.
  4. Add the ground beef to the pan and thoroughly break apart/mix around. Add broth if necessary.
  5. Add Tomato sauce after beef is cooked through and turn off the heat after 3 minutes.
  6. Layer pan mixture at the bottom of a baking dish, then add mashed potatoes on top.
  7. Cover the top layer of mashed potatoes with grated cheese, then broil on high for 2 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
  9. Lasts in the fridge for about 4 days
