Roasted Garlic Zucchini and Tomato

  • 2 zucchini cut in half lengthwise, then cut into ½-inch half moons
  • 2 cups quartered ripe tomatoes
  • ½ onion, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Combine zucchini, tomatoes, onion, garlic, and red pepper flakes in the prepared baking dish. Drizzle with olive oil; season with salt and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven until vegetables are tender and slightly golden, about 18 minutes. Remove from the oven; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and basil.
